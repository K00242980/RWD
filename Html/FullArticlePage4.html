Some Useful Websites

Author: K00242980 Date:5th November 2019

There are so many records available to help you find out information about your ancestors.These are some of the most useful websites where you can search records of.ie Marriage and Birth Records

National Archives : The National Archives was established on 1 June 1988 following the amalgamation of the State Paper Office (SPO) and the Public Record Office of Ireland (PROI). They also hold and secure the preservation of records of the Irish State and ensure appropriate arrangements for public access to archives.

Family Search : Feamily Search.Org is a nonprofit organization and a websites that offers genealogical records,education and sofftware. It is operated by the Church Of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints and it is closely connected with the Family History Department of this church.This organization is based in Salt Lake City,Utah,USA.

Ancestry : This website is the largest private online genealogy database. It allows users to create virtual family trees to trace their lineage, and get "hints" about who else could be their ancestors. It also contains historical records that can be connected to family members and used as genealogical evidence.

Irish Genealogy : s a website that allows users the opportunity to search a wide range of record sources in their search of their Irish Ancestry. This website also operates as a search portal that allows users to search the following record sources as well: 1901/1911 Census records and pre-1901 survivals.

Find My Past : This is a website set up by two experts in the field of family history: Eneclann, an award-winning research and publishing house with extensive experience in family history in Ireland, and Findmypast, a leading family history website, initially launched in the UK.
